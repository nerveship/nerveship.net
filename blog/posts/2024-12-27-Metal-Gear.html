METAL GEAR

I recently just finished Kojima Productions' 2015 effort: Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain, after going through the rest of the games in the series. I thought now might be a good time to finally try writing about art I've enjoyed.

It's a series I'm sure we're all familiar with in 2025, yet I didn't know too much about it when I started. I never knew any of the major plot points. All I understood was that it was a series that included espionage and politics.

This may go without saying, but the blog post will likely spoil most, if not all of the Metal Gear series. Go and play it before reading!

THE BEGINNING

Where else would you start, right?

For some reason this seems to be a point of contention within not only the Metal Gear community, but lots of other series too.

A lot of people started with Metal Gear Solid, which to an extent I understand. I didn't even realise Metal Gear (1987) and it's sequel Metal Gear 2: Solid Snake (1990) existed until I did preliminary research on the best way to get into the series. I cannot understate their importance to the overall plot however. Especially when it comes to my favourite character in the series...
