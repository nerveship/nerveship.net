Mail Gunner Carnst — February 20, 2026

As a new worker for the St. Carnst postal service, you arrive for your first shift.

"Yeah so, the autonomous mail system has been infected."

"By like... a virus?"

"Yeah, but not a computer virus."

"It's some sort of plant. We've been instructed to blast it away before handing the packages over."

"No clue what happens if we miss any, or, god forbid, miss all of them."

Save St. Carnst from the plant menace!

Care(taking) — April 22, 2025

A short experimental game created in 12 days.

You are the caretaker of a graveyard at world's end. Each day, five spirits show at a random grave on the plot. Only two may be freed from this world.

Your choices in this world do not matter.
