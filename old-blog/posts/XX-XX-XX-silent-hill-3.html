SILENT HILL 3 - We were put here on this earth, put here to feel joy.
DATE, TIME
Silent Hill 3 was developed by Team Silent and published by Konami. It was directed by Kazuhie Nakazawa, produced and composed by Akira Yamaoka, written by Hiroyuki Owaku and the lead artist was Hiroyuki Owaku. It released on the 3rd of July, 2003 in Japan for Sony's PlayStation 2.
